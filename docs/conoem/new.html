About Us:

Concrete OEM has been established by experienced Readymixed concrete professionals with over two decades of experience in working with some of the most prominent names in the Indian and International Readymixed concrete industry.
The founders bring in their rich experience and customer knowhow to establish a Readymixed concrete facility which aims at achieving customer delight.
We have a dynamic and energetic team to serve and fulfil your concrete needs.
We commenced our operations from Chennai from late 2017 after commission of our plant at Erikarai on the outer ring road.
Within a span of few months we have been able to associate with some of the big names in Chennai Construction Industry as a concrete producer of choice.

Concrete OEM has received the prestigious RMCPS certification awarded by QCI.

RMC Plant Certification Scheme (RMCPS) is a third party voluntary certification Scheme initiated by Quality Council of India (QCI) along with key stakeholders, i.e.Ready Mixed Concrete Manufacturers' Association (RMCMA), a Mumbai based non-profit industry organization of leading Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) producers in India; Building Materials & Technology Promotion Council (BMTPC) under Ministry of Housing & Urban Poverty Alleviation, Government of India and various other stakeholdes.
QCI envisaged that In the era of huge infrastructure development in the country, the launch of this scheme will not only be very useful for the RMC manufacturing industry to benchmark itself with quality certifications but will also provide consumer the RMC produced in a quality certified RMC Plant.
The scheme establishes a detailed Production Control Criteria for RMC, laying down the requirements on plant and equipment, key personnel, concrete mix design, production, testing facilities, control on quality of concrete ingredients, final product, delivery, control and maintenance of process control equipment etc. which the RMC Plant must comply in order to get certified under the scheme.
For more details please visit QCI Website

Our Products:

Apart from conventional readymixed concrete conforming to various grades of concrete, Concrete OEM also specialises in producing Concrete with special properties, each designed to cater to the requirement of the end user.
Concrete OEM also helps our customer in choosing the right product to meet the customer requirement.

High Strength Concrete
High Strength concrete products help our customer in achieving optimum member size and lower slab cycles, resulting in great project cost saving. Mixes are available up to M80 and early strength mixes can deliver about 70% strength in 24 - 72 hours depending upon the requirements
Light Weight Concrete
Light weight concrete can be used as filler whilst keeping your structural dead weight at minimum and giving economy in design. Available in densities of 1000 kg/cum to 1800 kg/cum
Self Compacting Concrete
Congested reinforcement or architectural requirements often make it difficult to use conventional compaction methods while placing concrete. Our SCC mixes provide the solutions, Our SCC mixes pass all parameters required for being truly called as SCC and are not just high flow mixes.
High Durability Concrete
Concrete deteriorates when in proximity or in an aggressive environment, Our Durability enhancing mixes help resist the various external attacks affecting the concrete and enhancing its life

Long Retention Concrete
Concreting for columns is often time consuming as being dependent on tower crane and manual placing. Conventional readymix concrete loses its workability quickly and this is even more crucial for High Cementitious mixes.
Concrete may be tampered to increase the workability ultimately affecting the strength. Columns are very important members of the structure, Rectification of defect is often time consuming and costly.
Our Long retention concrete mixes gives retention up to 5 hours ensuring required product quality without sacrificing workability.
Fibre Reinforced Concrete
Concrete has high compressive strength as we all know, however the flexural strength is almost 10% of the compressive strength. Our Fibre reinforced concrete mixes provide for requirements with higher flexural strength such as for pavements, tunnels, machine foundations.
Fibre reinforced concrete mixes also help enhance the abrasion resistance of concrete
Flowable Concrete
Flowing, Cohesive concrete for Applications in Slip-formwork, Mivan-formwork at a lower binder content than Self Compacting Concrete and lower price.
Flow range is 550 - 650 mm
Temperature Controlled Concrete
Mass foundation concreting and associated entrapment of the heat of hydration in the placed concrete poses challenges on the long term durability of concrete by inducing microcracks due to stresses induced due to an expansion at the core and contraction on external surface.
Our special concrete product uses unique blend of materials to keep core temperature low and ensure the temperature differential remains between the prescribed limits to ensure a durable foundation for your structure.
