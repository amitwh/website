About CEMCON

The Indian Concrete Institute is hosting it's Annual 2 Day Conference "CEMCON 2019" on the 13th & 14th December 2019. CEMCON 2019 is being orgainsed at Hotel Sheraton Grand, Raja-Bahadur Mill Road, Pune

The annual conference brings in together Technocrats, Academicians and Students from around India and Abroad

CEMCON 2018 was a widely successful event with over 300 plus delegates, sponsors and authors

The event garnered wide appreciation for the content and overall professional management by the delegates, authors and sponsors. You may catch all of the lectures of CEMCON 2018 on our YouTube Channel

We look forward to making CEMCON 2019 even better.
